Choosing the Right Time for Dubai Desert Safari

Select the Right Desert Safari Company

Dress Conservatively and Comfortably

The desert climate is hot, humid, and harsh. You will need lightweight and comfortable clothing. Also, closed-toe shoes are recommended for protection. Don't forget to bring a hat and sunglasses to shield yourself from the sun's rays. Also, sunscreen lotions and creams with some bug-repellant properties will be significant as well.

Stay Hydrated, Stay Healthy

Bring plenty of water to stay hydrated during your desert safari. The desert is a dry and humid environment that will take humidity away faster than most can replenish it. Also, while many operators provide water, bringing extra on hand ensures you remain refreshed throughout the adventure.
